SPAI — Secure Personal AI — is being built for people and small businesses who want an AI assistant that can learn, adapt, and help with real work without sending their files, memory, or workflows to cloud platforms.

The problem

Powerful AI should not require giving up control.

Cloud AI tools are useful, but many users and small businesses are uncomfortable sending private documents, workflows, conversations, and long-term memory to external platforms. Local AI is improving quickly, but today it is still too technical for most people to set up and use properly.

SPAI is designed to bridge that gap: a personal AI layer that makes local models, memory, tools, and automation feel like one assistant rather than a pile of technical components.

What SPAI is designed to do

A private assistant that can grow with your hardware and workflow.

01

Run locally

Use local AI models on supported consumer hardware so sensitive data can stay on the user’s own machine.

02

Route intelligently

Send simple tasks to fast models and heavier work to larger local models or additional compute nodes.

03

Remember with consent

Save preferences, projects, and important context under user control instead of relying on hidden cloud memory.

04

Use permissioned plugins

Add capabilities such as file reading, app opening, document work, voice, and learned workflows with clear permissions.

05

Support small businesses

Offer a lower-cost route for small teams that need private AI assistance without expensive enterprise AI contracts.

06

Stay user-owned

The long-term goal is simple: your assistant, your hardware, your memory, your tools, your control.

Who it is for

Built for people who want useful AI without becoming AI engineers.

Individuals & professionals

For privacy-conscious users, freelancers, creators, students, and professionals who want a personal AI assistant that adapts to them without needing to build a local AI stack by hand.

Small & medium businesses

For teams that want private AI help with documents, admin, research, customer support, and internal workflows without recurring enterprise AI costs or cloud data lock-in.
